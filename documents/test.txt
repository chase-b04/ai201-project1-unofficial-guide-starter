I seriously recommend The Regency (it’s on Terrance and Lemon) everytime someone is looking for a one bedroom. I think it’s about $1300-$1400, you do have to pay for electricity but I never paid more than $60 a month. And that was during the summer, during winter/spring my electricity bill was like $20-$30. Also do have to pay wifi I believe but the $30 plan a lot of places have is amazing, it use to work fine with three people i had at my place. The apartments are updated and modern, they offer unfurnished and furnished units. Management is amazing. You won’t have to worry about loud neighbors or parties since the apartment is a small one with like 30 units. I did not have a problem with bugs at all, not even during summer. Only downside is that there is no washer/dryer inside your apartment but they do have some on the property. It’s also quite close to campus, like less than a five minute walk.
